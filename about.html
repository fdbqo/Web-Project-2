About us

SurfSligo is a surf shopping website based out of County Sligo, Ireland. Our goal is to provide surfers with the best equipment at the best prices. We are a small team of surfers who are passionate about the sport and want to share our passion with the world. We are located in the heart of Sligo town, just a short drive from the beach. Our support is open 7 days a week to provide all the help necessary

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide surfers with great cost items and fast delivery, while remaining sustainable. All materials we use are sustainably made wherever possible.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate and experienced individuals who love surfing and the surf culture. We have a diverse team with a wide range of skills and experiences, which allows us to bring a unique perspective to our work.

John Doe, our CEO, is a seasoned surfer with over 20 years of experience in the industry. Jane Smith, our CTO, brings a wealth of technical knowledge and innovation to our team. Bob Johnson, our Marketing Manager, has a knack for connecting with our customers and understanding their needs.

We are all united by our love for surfing and our commitment to providing the best products and services to our customers. We believe in the power of community and are proud to be a part of the vibrant surf community in Sligo.
